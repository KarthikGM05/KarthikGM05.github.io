About Me

Who am I?



Hi! I'm Karthik G M. A graduate student in Computer Science & Engineering. As a fresher, I have a deep desire to traverse the mystical 🔮 world of Computer Science. I have a fierce passion for programming and wanna forge my own path in the industry. A self learner, eagerly willing to get the hang of new stuff all the time. Driven by morals, easy going and adaptable. I'm here to make a difference by adding values to the firms I work for, and to myself as I continue to grow - both personally & professionally.

I was introduced to coding back in 2015, when I joined pre-university. A facinated me initially went on writing C/C++ codes to solve simple tasks and explore the possibilities that could be achieved. I got duly encouraged in choosing a major in CS and since then, I have worked with multiple streams of technologies. That initial facination still exists and I keep up with new advancements, with the aim of becoming a panoramic developer.

I follow the belief that a strong theoretical foundation is essential for all the practicalities of work involved in this field as it helps focus on the intricacies involved. As an elocutionist, I influence people positively with my thoughts and one of my favorite activities is to educate and mentor others in the field so that they are able to realise their true potential.
